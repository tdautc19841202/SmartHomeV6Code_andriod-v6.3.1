Camera and cell phone should be under the same Wi-Fi. Choose the Wi-Fi with strong signals. Keep suitable distance with router. Enter correct Wi-Fi passwords, then the camera can connect internet quickly.

Following issues may result in failure of camera network configuration:

1. Check whether turn on Wi-Fi anti-loiter network mode which may cause the camera failed to connect with Internet. You can turn off anti-loiter network mode temporarily, and reset when camera connects with Internet successfully.

2. If your router is dual-brand router, please check whether the Wi-Fi your camera connected is 5GHz bands. Please connect the camera with Wi-Fi transmitting 2.4GHz bands.

3. If your Wi-Fi is bridge connected, suggest to use non-bridge connected Wi-Fi, because bridge connection may cause unstable network.
